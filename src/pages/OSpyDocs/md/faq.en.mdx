### Relation of the O-Spy and PageSpy? #difference

O-Spy is built on top of the capabilities of [PageSpy](/) and its plugin ecosystem. Specifically, it packages the following three dependencies:

- `@huolala-tech/page-spy-browser`: PageSpy's SDK for the Web environment, which enables offline mode in O-Spy;
- `@huolala-tech/page-spy-plugin-rrweb`: Records user interaction traces on the page;
- `@huolala-tech/page-spy-plugin-data-harbor`: Processes the data captured by PageSpy and provides export and other operations.
